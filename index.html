Welcome to The Media Landscape (MeLa) Lab.

What is The MeLa Lab?

The Media Landscape Lab is a multi-university group of researchers that collaborate on problems related to online news and multimedia. Our work ranges from studies of unsafe media platforms to disinformation in news articles. The goal of the lab is to provide an understanding of malicious information behavior across the web and to provide innovative interventions.
